About Me



Hi, I'm Ryan! I am in my final year of study at UNC Chapel Hill studying Political Science, Information Systems, and Entrepreneurship. I am currently working as a Product Management Intern with 3x3 Insights and as a Venture Fellow with Cofounders Capital. I've started and scaled two of my own ventures, Cardinal Direction Pac and Raise Technologies. I previously worked with Anthos Capital as a Private Equity Analyst, the Kenan Institute as a Researcher, and was a prior digital campaign manager for two North Carolina District Court Races. I am fortunate to have studied political economy and innovation in Singapore as well as Arabic language in Morocco. I am looking to pursue a operations or product-facing role in the fintech or enterprise SaaS space after graduating from UNC in May while continuing to work on various side projects.
